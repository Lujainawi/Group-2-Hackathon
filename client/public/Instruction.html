How to Play Stay Sharp

Learn how to identify and handle risky situations safely while navigating through different storylines.

Steps to Play:

  • Create your character and personalize your profile.
  • Choose a storyline to explore: Online Friendships, School Dynamics, or Community Involvement.
  • Make decisions at critical moments and learn from the outcomes.
  • Finish the game and review your feedback to improve your awareness.

Tips for Success:

  • Think carefully before sharing personal information.
  • Trust your instincts—if something feels off, it probably is.
  • Always seek help from a trusted adult if you’re unsure.
